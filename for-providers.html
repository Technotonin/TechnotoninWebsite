For Providers

A new category of mobility device.
Built for the clinical pathway.

PAWE converts any manual wheelchair to electric in under 10 seconds — no permanent modification, no new chair required. Designed from the ground up to fit how providers prescribe, supply, and bill.

Request an LOI conversation

DME Suppliers

PAWE is a lightweight, attachable power unit that fits your existing manual chair inventory. No new SKU complexity. Straightforward supply chain integration with a device designed for broad manual chair compatibility.

Rehab Hospitals & PTs

Recommend PAWE as a transitional or supplemental mobility solution for patients who are not yet candidates for a full power wheelchair. No tools, no fitting appointment — patients can attach and detach independently.

VA Centers

PAWE offers veterans a portable, independently operable power option that travels with them — no accessible vehicle required. Compatible with the manual chairs most commonly prescribed through VA supply channels.

Reimbursement

Designed with the billing pathway in mind.

PAWE is being developed with HCPCS code E0986 in mind — the existing code for power-operated vehicle components. We are working with reimbursement specialists to establish a clear billing pathway ahead of commercial launch.

We are actively seeking DME partners to participate in early access conversations that will inform our reimbursement and supply strategy. If your organization wants a seat at that table, we want to hear from you.
